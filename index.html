About This Website

Why this Anna University CGPA Calculator exists

CGPA Buddy was created for students who need a fast and reliable Anna University CGPA calculator when official portals are slow, unavailable, or under maintenance during high-traffic result periods. Instead of waiting for a website to load, students can instantly enter grades and credits and get a clean, accurate CGPA output.

The calculator follows Anna University grade-point logic and uses weighted credit computation, making it useful for semester planning, internal score tracking, and quick what-if analysis before final results are published.

How It Works

Simple steps to calculate your CGPA

  1. Add or edit subjects based on your semester subjects.
  2. Select the correct grade for each subject: S, A+, A, B+, B, or U.
  3. Enter the subject credit for each course.
  4. Click Calculate CGPA to get the weighted CGPA instantly.

Formula used: CGPA = sum(gradePoint x credits) / sum(credits)

FAQ

Frequently searched questions

Is this calculator only for Anna University?

It is primarily optimized for Anna University grade-point mapping. You can still use it for any weighted grade system with the same scale.

What if the university website is down?

You can use this tool anytime to estimate and verify CGPA locally in your browser without waiting for official result portals.

Can I calculate semester-wise CGPA quickly?

Yes. Enter the subjects and credits for that semester and click Calculate CGPA for an instant weighted result.
